Why Workaholic?

Workaholic is a simple, easy to use, and free way to track your workouts. It's designed to be used on your phone or computer, so you can track your workouts anywhere.

Workaholic is designed to be simple and easy to use. It's not designed to be a social media platform, or a community. It's designed to be a tool to help you track your workouts.

Workaholic is free to use, and always will be.
